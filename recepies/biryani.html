Biryani Recepie

Description

The ingredients known to improve the flavors of Biryani include saffron (kesar), fried onions, ghee, Kewra water, and rose water. Each of them adds a unique flavor, texture, and smell to the dish.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste

steps

  1. 3 tablespoons plain yogurt (Indian curd)
  2. 1¼ tablespoons ginger garlic paste
  3. ½ to 1 tablespoon garam masala (or biriyani masala)
  4. ½ teaspoon salt
  5. ¼ teaspoon ground turmeric
  6. ½ to 1 teaspoon red chili powder (reduce for kids)
  7. 1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional)
