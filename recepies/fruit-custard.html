Fruit Custard Recepie

Description

Fruit custard is a creamy concoction combines seasonal fruits with custard powder, milk, and luscious Nestl√© MILKMAID, creating a sweet and satisfying treat that is perfect for family gatherings, festive celebrations, or simply as a refreshing end to your meal.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Custard powder
  • Sugar
  • Warm milk
  • Vanilla

steps

  1. 2.5 cups of milk is a common starting point, but plant-based options like almond or soy milk can also be used.
  2. 3 tablespoons of custard powder (or cornstarch or arrowroot powder) are used as a thickening agent.
  3. 4-5 tablespoons of sugar are used to sweeten the custard.
  4. A small amount of warm milk (1/4 cup) is used to dissolve the custard powder before adding it to the main milk mixture.
  5. You can enhance the flavor with a splash of vanilla or almond extract.
