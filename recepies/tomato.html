Tomatoe Recepie

Description

Tomato kurma, tamatar ki sabzi, tomato kootu all are various ways of making a tomato curry in Indian cuisine. This tomato curry is the most basic and is made with pantry staples.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoe
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Spices

steps

  1. Heat oil in a Pan, add mustard seeds and crackle them.
  2. Then add curry leaves, chopped Onion and saute till translucent.
  3. Then add chopped tomatoes saute them for 5 minutes.
  4. Add sambar powder, salt and 1 small cup water mix it well and saute for a while.
